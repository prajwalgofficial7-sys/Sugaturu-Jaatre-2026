Lord Sri Rama — Maryada Purushottama

ಶ್ರೀ ರಾಮನ ಜೀವನ ಸಂದೇಶ

Life lessons from the Ramayana woven into each day of the festival

"Lord Rama — the seventh avatar of Vishnu, the embodiment of dharma — journeyed from the throne of Ayodhya through 14 years of forest exile, defeated the demon king Ravana, and returned triumphant to restore righteousness. His life teaches us that truth, love, courage, humility, and unwavering devotion are the highest human virtues. This festival at Sri Kodanda Ramaswami Temple, Sugaturu, is a living tribute to those eternal values."
⚖️
Dharma
Rama chose exile to honor his father's word — righteousness above personal comfort
💝
Prem
His devotion to Sita, his brothers, and all beings was pure and boundless
🦁
Courage
Fearless warrior who vanquished Ravana — strength always in service of truth
🌿
Humility
A king who embraced Shabari's berries and called Hanuman his dearest friend
🤝
Friendship
Cemented eternal bonds with Sugreeva, Vibhishana, and the forest tribes
